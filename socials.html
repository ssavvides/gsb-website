Social Power Hour
SPH is an event that gives CS graduate students an opportunity for to share their work and learn more about the work of others. Each participant will have a maximum of five minutes to present their research, using slides or other audio-visual aids. The audience will have an opportunity to give, anonymous, feedback via a Qualtrics survey, which GSB will provide.
